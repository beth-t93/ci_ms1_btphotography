Beth Travis is a photographer who creates work within the realms of documentary and landscape.

A large portion of her work is created using 35mm and medium format film, however she will also use digital techniques alongside to create something that looks and feels analogue, with that natural film grain that no filter can replicate, but can be easily displayed and distributed digitally.

As a photographer, Beth has also worked commerically, working as a wedding and event photographer, as well as a newborn and family photographer, and also some corporate work. It was doing this work that she realised she preffered working more as an artist, rather than working in the commercial world.

Research

A lot of the work is centered around specific themes.

A key theme is the landscape and how it changes over time, both in appearence and use. Documenting this change and exploring how people use the space now.

Another aspect of the work is exploring 'Non-place'. Places that are non-despcript, with no real identity can be found anywhere, many people pass through them daily without even realising.
